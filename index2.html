Synopsis

When James Bond's latest assignment goes terribly wrong, it leads to a dark turn of events: Undercover agents around the world are exposed, and MI6 is attacked, forcing M to relocate the agency. With MI6 now compromised inside and out, M turns to the one man she can trust: Bond. Aided only by field agent Moneypenny, Bond takes to the shadows and follows a trail to Silva, a man from M's past who wants to settle an old score.
